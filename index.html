Work Experience


Murimi Charles Johnson has a dynamic background in tech instruction, administration, and IT support. His role involves teaching both front-end and back-end development, fostering creativity and hands-on learning. His diverse experience reflects a strong foundation in technical training, educational mentorship, and administrative efficiency — making him a well-rounded professional in the IT and education sectors.
